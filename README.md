# 217 Hotlines | Public Safety Database

### Our Mission
217 Hotlines is a community organization led by teens and young adults (ages 14+ and 18+). We are dedicated to protecting the youth of Roblox and Discord by providing education, identifying predatory behavior, and assisting in the reporting process to keep digital spaces safe.

### How We Work
This repository serves as a transparent record of public safety alerts. 
* All information is verified by our team before being posted.
* We focus on platform integrity and user safety.
* We work independently to support the Terms of Service of the platforms we monitor.

### Database Protocol
The data is stored in `database.json`. This is intended for use by server owners and moderators to cross-reference known risks and maintain community safety.
